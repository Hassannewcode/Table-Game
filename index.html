    Crossy Road

    Developer: Hipster Whale  -  517 607 plays

    The Road Crossy phenomenon arrives on browser! The principle: make cross the road to a chicken! In theory it sounds simple, but in practice it will be quite a trip. Indeed, our brave pet will go through crowded car highways, rails where trains run at high speed or cross rivers with the help of logs. The game uses the principle of the famous Frogger, released in 1981, and the goal is to travel the longest distance possible trying to beat his record to each test. It is also possible to unlock the other colorful characters.
